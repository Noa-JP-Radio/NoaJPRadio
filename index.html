This is not a class.

I don't teach grammar rules. I don't give tests. Because you don't need another textbook.

What you need is exposure.

Babies don't study grammar. They listen. They observe. They feel the language before they understand it. That is how we naturally learn.

Noa JP Radio is designed to be your daily dose of real Japanese. It might feel fast. It might feel difficult. That's okay.
